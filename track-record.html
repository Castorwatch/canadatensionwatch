💡 What is a prediction?

The Beaver watches real signals (urgent resales, auto theft…) and calls in advance where the score is heading over the next 4 to 8 weeks. It is a dated, public bet that cannot be erased.

📊 Predicted score — where the Beaver thinks it is going (out of 100)
📍 Score at time — where it was when the bet was made
⏳ Verification — the date we will know if it was right
✅ Later — CORRECT or WRONG, never hidden
Example: "predicted 41, score at time 32" = the Beaver bets stress will climb from 32 to 41. See you on the verification date.
